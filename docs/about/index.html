Duncan Tai Bennett

I am a recent Mathematics PhD graduate from the University of Arizona. My dissertation is titled Computer-Assisted Proofs of Chaos in the Long Short-Term Memory Model and I was advised by Dr Marek Rychlik . My current work involves using creating production ready machine learning systems that leverage mature mathematics such as causal inference, stochastic processes, persistent homology and more.
