Duncan Tai Bennett

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the applied mathematics department of ENSTA from the Institut Polytechnique de Paris. I am also affiliated with the combinatorial optimization team in the CEDRIC lab at the Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers. I work with Andrea Simonetto, and Sourour Elloumi. My research focuses on quantum algorithms for optimization problems, algebraic combinatorics and cooperative game theory.

In September 2026, I will join the LIP6 at Sorbonne Université as an assistant professor, in the Décision team.
