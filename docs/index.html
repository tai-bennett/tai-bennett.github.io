Duncan Tai Bennett

I am a Mathematics PhD from the University of Arizona specializing in dynamical systems and machine learning. My research analyzes machine learning models as dynamical systems and interprets the dynamical properties in the ML setting. My dissertation is titled Computer-Assisted Proofs of Chaos in the Long Short-Term Memory Model and I was advised by Dr. Marek Rychlik.
