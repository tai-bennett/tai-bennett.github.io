My work belongs to three overlapping fields: quantum information and algorithms, algebraic combinatorics, and game theory. I participate in the HQI initiative , from the French national quantum plan, which funds my postdoc together with the Fondation Mathématique Jacques Hadamard .

Areas of interest Supervision Publications Communication Collaborators

Areas of interest

Currently, I am working on

  • algebraic and combinatorial structures in game theory and decision theory,
  • quantum algorithms for algebraic problems.
